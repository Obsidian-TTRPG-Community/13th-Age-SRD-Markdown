# High Elf

## Racial Bonus

+2 Int or +2 Cha

## Racial Power

***Highblood Teleport***
Once per battle as a move action, place yourself in a nearby location you can see.

***High Elf Champion Feat***
Deal damage equal to twice your level to one enemy engaged with you before or after you teleport.
