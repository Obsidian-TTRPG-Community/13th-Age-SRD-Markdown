## Half-Orc

### Racial Bonus

+2 Str or +2 Dex

### Racial Power

#### Lethal

Once per battle, reroll a melee attack and use the roll you prefer as the result.

#### Half-Orc Champion Feat

If the *lethal* attack reroll is a natural 16+, you can use *lethal* again later this battle.
