## Other Damage Types

Different monsters and characters may be resistant or vulnerable to  
various types of damage, including:

- Acid
- Cold
- Fire
- Force
- Holy
- Lightning
- Negative energy
- Poison
- Psychic
- Thunder (sonic energy)
